# Code of Conduct

Marvin should be a practical, respectful project for people who want clearer
cloud cost reporting.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but be specific when something needs to change.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect different levels of experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors
when behavior damages the project or community.

To report a concern, use the contact method listed in `SECURITY.md`.
